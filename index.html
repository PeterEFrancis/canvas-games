Traffic Lights

In traffic lights, opponents alternate clicking squares on an m x n board and by doing so, progress squares forward (empty, green, yellow, red). The board begins empty and once a square is red, it cannot be changed. The players race to make a row, column, or diagonal of three consecutive squares of the same color.
